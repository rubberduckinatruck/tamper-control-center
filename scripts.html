📜 Tampermonkey Script List

📚 Teaching Tools

🧑‍🏫 Teaching

  • Auto-Fill Gradebook Comments: Quickly fill in pre-written grading phrases into your SIS or LMS.
  • Google Form Clipboard Copier: Copies student responses into clipboard-friendly format.
  • Student Name Highlighter: Highlights student names across platforms to reduce grading fatigue.
  • Auto-Link Lesson Resources: Automatically attach lesson links to your Google Classroom or LMS pages.
  • Classroom Timer Overlay: Displays a timer on instructional pages during lessons or assessments.

🧾 Recordkeeping

  • Incident Log Timestamp Tool: Log student or classroom incidents with exact timestamps and notes.
  • PDF Parent Contact Exporter: Export all logged parent contacts into a printable PDF.
  • Auto Email Logger: Saves a copy of school-related emails to a local or synced archive.
  • Meeting Reflection Saver: Prompts reflective questions after meetings or difficult interactions.
  • Daily Logbook Template Inserter: Inserts a templated daily record sheet into documentation tools.

✊ Union Tools

📣 Organizer

  • Petition QR Generator: Creates a printable or digital QR code linked to your petition or form.
  • Action Heatmap Visualizer: Visual map of which sites or individuals have taken union actions.
  • Flyer Template Switcher: Instantly swap flyer templates or upload custom designs.
  • Mass Email Composer: Builds multi-recipient union email templates with merge tags.
  • Live Signup Form Watcher: Tracks new signups in shared forms and flags duplicates.

👥 Site Rep

  • Issue Intake Button: Quick form launcher to track site-level concerns or requests.
  • Site Rep Meeting Prep Sheet: Auto-generates a to-do checklist before rep council or admin meetings.
  • Support Request Auto-Categorizer: Sorts issues into categories like workload, retaliation, or SPED.
  • 1-on-1 Checklist Generator: Produces printable/member-ready checklist for sit-down meetings.
  • Grievance Status Panel: Displays and tracks grievance status visually on a single page.

🛡️ Defense

  • Weingarten Warning Auto-Overlay: Pops up your rights when admin meetings are mentioned.
  • Retaliation Watch Trigger: Flags patterns of administrative retaliation after union activity.
  • Contract Clause Lookup: Fast lookup for CBA language by topic.
  • Red Flag Email Highlighter: Warns when emails contain investigatory, disciplinary, or threatening tones.
  • Defense Report Timestamp Tool: Logs events with timestamped records for future grievance filing.

🧠 Officer

  • Officer Digest Generator: Creates a summary digest of all rep/site activity for the week.
  • Site Rotation Planner: Assigns reps, observers, or tasks in a rotating calendar.
  • Calendar Event Scraper: Pulls events from shared union calendar to a private spreadsheet.
  • Site Activity Heatmap: Shows levels of member engagement per site or department.
  • Progress Tracker Grid: Visual tracker of campaign or issue progress across school sites.

📜 Board Watcher

  • BoardDocs Agenda Extractor: Parses meeting agendas and pulls out relevant policies.
  • BoardDocs Attachment Downloader: Downloads and organizes all attachments from a posted agenda.
  • YouTube Transcript Tagger: Tags timestamps on livestreams where important events occur.
  • Board Meeting Log Inserter: Injects a comment log overlay into board stream viewing.
  • Hash Checker for Minutes: Checks if minutes have been modified since upload.

📉 Pattern Tracker

  • Behavioral Tactic Timeline Builder: Creates a timeline of repeated district behavior (e.g., delays, overreach).
  • Strategic Language Highlighter: Highlights legal gaslighting, ‘respectful dialogue’, or coded district talk.
  • Pattern Heatmap Overlay: Shows frequency of patterns over time and by document/source.
  • Gaslighting Tag Annotator: Lets you mark and log examples of dismissal or contradiction.
  • Overreach Incident Counter: Tracks when the district exceeds its contractual or legal limits.

🗃️ Archiving Tools

  • One-Click Archive Button: Sends the current page to Wayback Machine and archive.today instantly.
  • Offline Page Saver: Downloads the page content into a .html archive for offline use.
  • Compare Page Versions: Tells you what changed between two versions of the same page.
  • Auto Wayback Submitter: Submits target pages to Wayback on a schedule or trigger.
  • Archive.today Backup Trigger: Queues up archive.today snapshots with optional delay.

📑 Verification Tools

  • Quote Anchor Generator: Creates reference-safe links to specific quotes or clauses.
  • Document Hash Fingerprinter: Produces a cryptographic hash to verify document hasn't changed.
  • Clause Reference Binder: Links a pulled clause to its original contract/article source.
  • Contract Excerpt Exporter: Lets you grab and bundle key language for external reference.
  • Revision Log Inserter: Appends an edit history log to a tracked policy or contract section.

🕵️ Privacy Assist

  • EXIF Auto-Stripper: Warns if you're uploading a file with embedded image metadata.
  • Upload Metadata Warning: Alerts you before uploading files with sensitive data (like DOCX revisions).
  • Bookmark with Metadata Saver: Saves author/date/keywords along with your saved link.
  • Document Privacy Previewer: Lets you preview what metadata is still inside before upload.
  • Sharing Risk Highlighter: Flags file types with high privacy risk (e.g., layered PDFs, GPS photos).

🧰 System Tools

🛠️ System

  • Script Toggle Panel: Main script toggler — turns scripts on/off by hat from the Control Center.
  • Dashboard Injector: Injects floating menu into approved pages.
  • Update Checker: Checks if your installed scripts match the current repo version.
  • Script Backup Exporter: Exports your script setup to local backup or GitHub.
  • Settings Sync Manager: Pushes/pulls your configuration across browsers.

📊 Research + Data Tools

📁 Research Tools

  • Fuzzy Term Finder: Finds and highlights similar but non-identical terms (e.g., equity/equality).
  • Table Formatter & Exporter: Cleans and exports HTML tables to CSV with readable formatting.
  • File-to-Text Converter: Opens small PDFs/DOCX and shows plain-text preview in-browser.
  • Smart Table Merger: Combines similar data tables on one page for better comparison.
  • Keyword Density Scanner: Counts how often terms appear and where.

📊 Data Tools

  • Inline Chart Generator: Turns numbers into quick graphs (bar/line/pie) right on the page.
  • Multi-Table Exporter: Grabs all data tables on a page into one multi-tab spreadsheet.
  • “Why I Saved This” Autofill: Prompts you to jot a reason when saving a link — helps later recall.
  • Interactive Data Summary: Summarizes tables into averages, percentages, and top-line numbers.
  • Dynamic Stat Block Inserter: Inserts key stat summaries into your notes or pages.

💲 Price Tracking Tools

💸 Price Tracking

  • CamelCamelCamel Injector: Embeds historical price charts into Amazon listings.
  • Unit Price Auto-Calculator: Shows price per oz/lb/item next to bulk product listings.
  • Alternate Sellers Finder: Finds other stores selling the same item via UPC or product ID.
  • Buy This Later List: One-click save wishlist items into a hidden rotating list.
  • Amazon Tracker Overlay: Displays price drops and watch status directly on Amazon pages.
